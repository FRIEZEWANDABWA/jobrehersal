# AI leadership in regulated and scaled environments

## Governance that enables speed

The best programs pair clear policy with self-service patterns: approved model routes, logging standards, evaluation gates for customer-facing features, and lightweight exception paths for experiments.

## Practical adoption

- Start with workflows that already produce structured data and measurable quality.
- Invest in evaluation harnesses before you invest in more model parameters.
- Treat prompt changes like code changes: versioning, review, and rollback.

## Risk and ethics without buzzwords

Be ready to discuss bias, misuse, data residency, and intellectual property in plain language, tied to controls your organization can operate.

## Executive framing

Position AI as a capability layer that changes unit economics and customer experience, not as a science fair bolted onto the side of the business.
