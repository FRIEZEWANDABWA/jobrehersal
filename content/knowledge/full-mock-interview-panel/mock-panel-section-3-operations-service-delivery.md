### 9. How do you ensure high availability of IT services?

**Elite answer**

“I design for failure rather than assuming uptime.

That means redundancy, proactive monitoring, capacity planning, disciplined incident management, and tested recovery readiness.

I focus heavily on removing single points of failure—backup connectivity, clustered firewalls, validated backups, and strong vendor accountability.

I also use Root Cause Analysis aggressively because repeated incidents usually signal architectural weakness, not bad luck.

High availability is not preventing all failure—it is ensuring failure does not stop the business.”
